New this year: a faster way to complete your matching-funds receipt.

Berkeley's public financing program asks contributors to sign a short receipt so your donation can be matched 6-to-1. Traditionally that meant printing, signing, scanning, and emailing it back. This form does the same thing online — fill it out, sign on your screen, and submit, no printer or scanner needed. Prefer the paper route? , print and sign it, then email it to DariaForBerkeley@proton.me.
